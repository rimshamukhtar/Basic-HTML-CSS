Political Instability

The political situation in Pakistan right now is quite unstable and divided. Ever since Imran Khan was removed as Prime Minister in 2022, there’s been a lot of political tension. His party, PTI, still has a lot of support from the public, while the current government, led by PML-N and PPP, is trying to maintain control.

Economic Struggles

On top of that, the economy is really struggling with high inflation and debt, making things harder for everyone.

Election Concerns

With the next elections coming up, people are worried about whether they’ll be fair and transparent. The uncertainty around what will happen next is making the situation even more tense.
